# DESIGNING AND DEVELOPMENT OF WIDEBAND VHF/UHF STRIP DISCONE ANTENNA (70 – 700 MHz)

## Abstract
This paper presents the design, simulation, construction, and performance evaluation of a low-cost wideband strip discone antenna fabricated from recycled aluminum materials, targeting VHF/UHF television broadcast reception (473–700 MHz) and amateur radio frequency transceiver operation (136–350 MHz). The antenna geometry was derived from established ARRL Antenna Book formulations, cross-validated through both online computational tools and manual calculations and subsequently optimized via iterative parametric analysis within the MATLAB Antenna Toolbox. The optimized physical prototype features a cone height of 300 mm, a lower cone diameter of 400 mm, a disc diameter of 360 mm, and a disc-cone spacing of 5 mm. Simulation results indicate a minimum S11 of −19.5 dB, a calculated VSWR of 1.1081, and a wideband resistance response of 45–55 Ω over 250–700 MHz, confirming excellent impedance matching with the 50 Ω feed system. The antenna exhibits a peak gain of 3.06 dBi at 600 MHz and an omnidirectional azimuth radiation pattern characteristic of discone structures. Physical performance testing using a modified Readability-Signal Strength-Tone (RST) evaluation system demonstrated reception of up to 42 digital and 9 analog television channels in an outdoor high-elevation environment. Radio frequency transmission tests showed the strip discone antenna achieved an overall RST readability mean of 4.71 and a signal strength mean of 7.29, substantially outperforming the transceiver's built-in antenna (readability: 1.67; signal strength: 2.92). These results confirm the viability of the strip discone antenna as a cost-effective, high-performance solution for simultaneous VHF/UHF television reception and amateur radio transmission, with direct implications for improving communication infrastructure access in resource-constrained environments.

**Keywords:** Discone antenna, impedance matching, MATLAB simulation, omnidirectional antenna, RST evaluation, strip antenna, UHF reception, VHF/UHF broadband antenna, wideband antenna design.
