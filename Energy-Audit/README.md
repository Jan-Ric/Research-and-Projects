# ISO-Based Energy Audit & 10-Year Demand Forecasting for Barangay Lower Bicutan

## Problem Statement
I answered: "Which energy interventions yield the fastest cost recovery for Barangay Lower Bicutan Hall, and what does the 10-year demand trajectory require us to plan for now?"

## Audience
This project is for barangay officials and local government unit (LGU) energy managers making capital allocation decisions on energy efficiency upgrades.

## KPI or Key Metric
The main metric I track is monthly energy savings (kWh and Php) per intervention, with simple payback period (months) as the prioritization signal and Prophet-forecasted peak demand (kWh/month) as the forward planning anchor.

## Data Source
I explored Meralco electric bills from Barangay Lower Bicutan Hall, Taguig City (February 2022 to November 2024), collected directly via photocopied billing statements during on-site audit.

## Result and Impact
Delivered a ranked intervention plan and a 10-year demand forecast using Prophet on 34 months of Meralco billing data. Full implementation of LED retrofit, inverter AC replacement, and phantom load elimination cuts monthly consumption by 1,220.84 kWh (24%) and saves Php 161,121/year against a 2.625%-error-validated audit baseline. The forecast projects peak demand growing from 5,819 kWh/month in 2025 to 9,471 kWh/month by 2035 (74% increase), quantifying the capacity risk the barangay must plan for within the decade.
